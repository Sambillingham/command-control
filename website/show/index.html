Command Control

An interactive physical computing game created by Sam Billingham and inspired by Spaceteam

You need 3 players. Press the big red button to start.

Each player takes control over one area of the ship using a control box. You will receive instructions from the ships internal system at the top of each control station.

Your system may be asking for a control from another area of the ship, you must work as a team to activate the correct switch, dial or slider wherever it may be. Keep the ship running as long as you can.

Activating the wrong control or running out of time will damage the ship.

Each round your ship will repair itself slightly but doing so scrambles the control systems and your input naming system will be changed.
