What is command control?

Command Control is a group gaming experience that focuses on creating interactions between. Players work together to stop a spaceship falling apart as it travels through hyperspace. Each player controls an area of the ship and must relay instructions to other players while simultaneously acting on instructions they receive.

Why did I make Command Control?

  • Group dynamics
  • Interactions created during party games
  • make people sound silly
